FAQ's

  • Who can download the app? Members working out of Phoenix campuses can download and access the exclusive offered on the app.
  • Why should I download? Phoenix is commited to offer ou an exemplary experience at its building and accessing the smart building services through the app is a step in that direction. Moreover, in the challenging Covid-19 times, the app will be means for you to get first hand runtime information about the measures being taken for your saftey and comfort.
  • How to reach to Converz team for any request or queries? We are always all ears for your feedback and suggestions. Please use the Service Request feature on the app and raise a ticket under the relevant category.
  • Do I need the app to invite visitors? It is best to use the Invite Visitor feature on the app to pre-invite your guests and save them the hassle of waiting. However, a contactless system has been introduced in the visitor lobby where the visitor can scan a QR code and fill in their details through a web link. All visitors will be allowed entry only after their Health Declaration form meets our safety guidelines.
